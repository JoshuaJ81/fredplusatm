why have an atm machine?

a few benefits to hosting an ATM machine in your business:



1. Increased Traffic To Your Business

Using an ATM machine outdoors can be risky. Once customers learn there is an ATM "inside" your business, this could lead to an increase of people who prefer to withdraw cash indoors.



2. An Increase In Customer Loyalty

Hosting an ATM machine in your business leads to an increase in sales. Period. Once customers have cash in hand, they're readily available to spend it on your products and/or services.


3. An Increase In Purchase of Goods and Services

Customers want to feel safe. Therefore, providing customers a safe means to withdraw cash enhances their loyalty to your place of business.



4. Monthly Cash Flow For Your Business

An increase in both traffic and sales equals more cash flow for your business. And, too, the monthly ATM Machine transaction profits are an additional source of cash for your business.
